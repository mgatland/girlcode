Download time

Download and install node.js (choose the option that says 'LTS' and 'Recommended For Most Users').

While that downloads, let's talk about server-side development.

Client-side code:

  • Runs on your computer
  • Shows stuff on your screen
  • Can't affect anyone else

Server-side code:

  • Decides what to give the client
  • Can share content with different people
  • Can't be messed with by users

Servers

Your computer (the client) asks for things from a server:

"Find this server and ask for this thing"

GET McDonalds/cheeseburger

GET and Post

GET: ask the server to give you something

POST: ask the server to save something
(like a facebook post)

In both cases, the client sends a request and the server replies with a response.
