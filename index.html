What happens at Girl Code?

Ten students meet once a week in Auckland to create a new website together.

Girl Code is for beginners with no previous experience. At the end of the course, you'll have a finished project to show your friends and family.

Wait, what am I signing up for?

You're going to build a "web application" (website!) with 2 experienced mentors and 9 other girls.

You don't need any previous experience. We will teach you what you need to know.

We meet on Thursday nights, from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM. You should attend all 8 classes.

As well as coming to class, you'll need to reserve 3 hours each week to work on the project at home.

Who are you?

Alice

Alice is passionate about helping women succeed in STEM. She's taught at and attended loads of coding workshops, and knows what works and what's still being figured out.

Years of improv comedy training make Alice an expert at getting groups of people collaborating creatively.

Matthew

Matthew has run coding workshops for Auckland Libraries, FutureInTech, OMG Tech!, Gather Workshops and also runs a weekly Code Club where he tests new material.

Alice and Matthew both work on Pop-Up Arcade, an inclusive space to play NZ-made video games, and previously worked on the Deadline Club, a weekly meetup that helped busy professionals make progress on their personal projects.
